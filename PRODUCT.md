# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Insurance agents and agency leaders ("CEO-agents") who manage their own book of
business. Their day is a sequence of tasks: birthday and renewal follow-ups,
chasing new leads before they go cold, recruiting and coaching sub-agents, and
recording notes after every customer touch. They work both at a desk and on the
move between client meetings, so the same data must feel native on a phone, a
tablet, and a wide monitor. Content is bilingual (Thai + English).

## Product Purpose

InsureFlow is a personal command center that tells an agent the single most
important thing to do next and lets them act on it in one tap. It unifies the
"Today" priority feed, customer records, the lead pipeline, and the agent-
recruiting pipeline over what is conceptually a set of Google Sheets. Success is
an agent who never misses a renewal or birthday, never lets a lead go cold, and
trusts the app enough to run their whole day from it.

## Brand Personality

Premium and executive. Calm confidence, not hype. Dark navy canvas with a
restrained gold accent; the product should feel like a private, high-end tool
rather than a mass-market CRM. Voice is warm and advisory — embodied by the
tagline "ดูแลลูกค้าด้วยความเข้าใจ ไม่เร่งขาย" (care for customers with
understanding, don't rush the sale). Three words: composed, trustworthy,
refined.

## Anti-references

- Loud SaaS dashboards drowning in charts, gauges, and gradient hero-metric cards.
- Generic Material/Bootstrap admin templates with flat gray tables and stock blue.
- Salesy, high-pressure "crush your quota" gamification.
- Toy-like over-rounded, candy-colored fintech aesthetics.

## Design Principles

- **Next action, not a data dump.** Every screen surfaces the one thing to do now and makes acting on it a single gesture.
- **Native on every screen.** Phone, tablet, and desktop each get a layout that fits the context — not one layout scaled up or down.
- **Restraint signals premium.** Gold and motion are earned accents reserved for state and primary actions, never decoration.
- **Calm over urgent.** Guide and reassure; never nag or pressure the agent (or, by extension, their customer).
- **Trust through consistency.** Same affordances screen to screen so the tool disappears into the task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large text/UI ≥3:1 against the dark canvas.
- Fully keyboard operable with visible focus states on every interactive element.
- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` with crossfade/instant alternatives for all animations.
- Touch targets ≥44×44px on coarse-pointer (touch) devices; hover affordances gated to fine-pointer devices.
- Bilingual Thai/English typography must remain legible at all sizes.
